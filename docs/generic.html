Over 50 best practice guides were released over the course of seven Model Edition releases. These best practice guides optimized common Revenue Cycle processes such as charge reconciliation, denials management, and end of month close to name a few. This allowed staff to be more efficient, productive, and increased data validation. Incorporating analytics and reporting into these daily, weekly, and monthly tasks ensured timely revenue and earlier error detection.
